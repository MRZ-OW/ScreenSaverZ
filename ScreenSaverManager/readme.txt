**Screensaver Manager for Kindle**
Author: TG @Shalom_Kir, @Made.by.Kir
Version: 2.1
English localization: @wellthatsnothing (Discord, also @Life)
Tested on: Kindle 11 (2022, KT5, C2V2L3), firmware 5.17.1.0.3 with WinterBreak
A web page is available for creating your own screensavers in the popular resolutions:
https://madebykir.github.io/kindle_custom_ScreenSaver/

Supported device resolutions:
- 600x800    Kindle Touch and other devices with this resolution
- 758x1024   Kindle Paperwhite and other devices with this resolution
- 1072x1448  Kindle 11 and other devices with this resolution
- 1236x1648  Kindle Paperwhite 5 and other devices with this resolution
- 1264x1680  Kindle Paperwhite 6 and other devices with this resolution

1. Download the extension archive and unzip it.
2. Connect your Kindle to your PC and copy the ScreenSaverManager folder into the "extensions" folder.
3. Place your prepared images into the ScreenSaverManager/screens/ folder.
   Format: PNG (8-bit grayscale)
   Names:  bg_ss00.png, bg_ss01.png, and so on
   Resolution: 1072x1448 px (for Kindle 11) - use the resolution that matches your device
4. The backup_screensavers folder already contains the original Kindle KT5 screensavers.
5. Open KUAL -> select Screensaver Manager, then choose:
   1. Create Backup   - saves a backup of your original screensavers.
   2. Restore Backup  - restores the original screensavers stored in ScreenSaverManager/backup_screensavers.
   3. Update screensavers - replaces the stock screensavers with your images placed in ScreenSaverManager/screens/.
   4. Disable Ads     - disables the ad screensavers on your Kindle. A reboot is required for this to take effect.
                        After that, ad screensavers will not appear again after a reboot.
   5. Enable Ads      - brings the ad screensavers back. A reboot is required for this to take effect.
   6. About the Author - information about the author, extension version, and release date.
